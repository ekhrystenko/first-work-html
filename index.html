half-life: episode 1
The first episode of the most popular game of the genre, in which you will find the five most interesting chapters, each of which is unique in its own way. From the first seconds of the game you will be thrown into the real thick of events that develop at an incredible speed and will definitely not let you get bored. You must immediately leave your hometown, which is about to be subject to destruction along with the stronghold of the Alliance.

half-life: episode 2
A new, improved and final part of a series of games where you will find yourself in incredible events that will send you to a fantasy world with a lot of interesting adventures. This part will continue the history of previous episodes, it will begin with the ending of the first episode. half-life alyx

Game events take place between Half-Life and Half-Life 2: Alix Vance and her father Eli lead early resistance against the Alliance and the brutal occupation of the Earth. The memory of the defeat in the Seven-Hour War is still fresh. In the shadow of the Citadel - the fortress of the Alliance - the inhabitants of City-17 learn to live under the rule of the invaders. Among the few surviving inhabitants, the two most inventive scientists of the Earth: Dr. Eli Vance and his daughter Alix - the founders of the nascent resistance.
